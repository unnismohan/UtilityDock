Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 13, 2026

1. Data Privacy

UtilityDock is built with a "Privacy First" philosophy. All developer tools provided on this platform run 100% client-side in your browser. We do not transmit, store, or process your input data on our servers.

2. Google AdSense

We use Google AdSense to serve advertisements. Google uses cookies to serve ads based on your previous visits to our website or other websites. Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to you based on your visit to our sites and/or other sites on the Internet.

You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Ads Settings.

3. Log Data

Like many site operators, we may collect information that your browser sends whenever you visit our Site ("Log Data"). This Log Data may include information such as your computer's Internet Protocol ("IP") address, browser type, browser version, the pages of our Site that you visit, the time and date of your visit, and other statistics.

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us via GitHub.
